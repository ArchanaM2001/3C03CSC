Overview

Leukemia is a broad term for cancers of the blood cells.The type of leukemia depends on the type of blood cell that becomes cancer and wheather it grows quickly or slowly.Leukemia occurs most often in adults older than 55,but it is also the most common cancer in children younger than 15.

Symptoms

  1. Fever or chills
  2. Persistance fatigue,weakness
  3. Frequent or severe infections
  4. Losing weight without trying
  5. Swollen lymph nodes,enlarged liver or spleen
  6. Easy bleeding or bruising
  7. Recurrent nosebleeds
  8. Tiny red spots in the skin
  9. Exessive sweating,especially at night
  10. Bone pain or tenderness
