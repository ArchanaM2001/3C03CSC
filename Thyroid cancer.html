Overview

These are four main types of thyroid cancer.These are papillary,follicular,medullary,anaplastic.Papillary is the most common type.The four types differ in how aggressive they are.Thyroid cancer that is found at an early stage can often be treated successfully.

Symptoms

  1. A lump(nodule)that can be felt through the skin on your neck
  2. Changes to your voice,including increasing hoarseness
  3. Difficulty in swallowing
  4. Pain in your neck and throat
  5. Swollen lymph nodes in your neck
